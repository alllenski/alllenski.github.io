Unsolved Business

One of the levels.

Unsolved Business is a game that aims to promote awareness for proper waste management. It was made for our first capstone project which required us to create a game and research that would satisfy our school and partner organization. It's a sokoban style puzzle game for Android devices. It features 17 levels and 3 different kinds of levels.

I was responsible for the level design and art in the game.
