The Weeks After

Banner art.

The Weeks After is a point and click adventure game we made for our second capstone. It draws inspiration from games like The Secret of Monkey Island. The story revolves around Adam, a detective, taking a case that spirals into something more.

I was responsible for the art in the game.

Right now only "chapter one" is completed but perhaps we will pick up this project again in the future.
