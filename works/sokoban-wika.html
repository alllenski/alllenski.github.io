Sokoban Wika

Sokoban with a Baybayin twist.

Made for a school project in high school

> Play here!

Sokoban Wika (there's no actual title) is a sokoban style game with a Baybayin twist. Baybayin is a Philippine script used before the colonization of the Philippines. The player must arrange the boxes in order to form the correct Filipino word based on the hint given.

This was my first official step into game development. I did everything except for the music which was done by my brother.
